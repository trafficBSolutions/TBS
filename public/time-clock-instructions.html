HOW TO VIEW YOUR WEEKLY HOURS

Traffic & Barrier Solutions, LLC

1. Go to www.trafficbarriersolutions.com

Click "Employee Login" and sign in.

Email and password are on the dry erase board.

2. Scroll down to "Enter PIN to view hours"
3. Type your 4-digit PIN and click "View My Weekly Hours"
4. Your hours for the week (Saturday – Friday) will show up.

✔ You can check your hours from your phone — you don't need to be at the shop.

✔ Clocking in/out must be done on the tablet at the shop.

✔ Do NOT share your PIN with anyone.
