⏰ HOW TO CLOCK IN & OUT

Traffic & Barrier Solutions, LLC

CLOCK IN:

1. Tap your name on the tablet.

2. Pick what job you are doing today from the list.

3. Type your PIN.

4. Tap "Clock In".

CLOCK OUT:

1. Tap your name on the tablet.

2. Type your PIN.

3. Tap "Clock Out".

RULES:

• Do NOT clock in or out for anyone else.

• Do NOT share your PIN.

• If wifi is down or power is out, clock in anyway — it still works.

• If you have any questions or concerns or forgot your PIN, contact Bryson (706) 263-0175 or Salvador (706) 659-5468.
